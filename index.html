Robinhood Chain · id 4663

Paste a token address. It reads the contract bytecode, the Uniswap v4 hook, the holder set, and the canonical stock-token registry — and shows you the evidence behind every flag, not just a number.

RPC is read-only (eth_call / eth_getLogs). Nothing is signed or sent. If the default endpoint rate-limits, drop in a QuickNode / Alchemy / dRPC URL.

What this can and can't see. Contract-capability, hook-permission, LP-position, holder-concentration and impersonation checks run entirely from public reads and are shown with evidence. A definitive honeypot sell test requires forking chain state and simulating a buy→sell round trip — that's a server/anvil step, so the contract module flags capabilities that enable honeypots rather than claiming a confirmed sell block. Not financial advice; verify before you trade.

Robinhood Chain uses first-come-first-served sequencing (no priority-fee reordering) and screens sanctioned addresses at the sequencer. Selectors and the Transfer topic embedded here were derived from a keccak-256 implementation checked against known test vectors.
